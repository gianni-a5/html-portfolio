Best Movies of All Time According to Gianni

Here is a list of my top 5 favourite films, in not particular order...

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

Beautifully artistic in every way. Funny, heart-warming, and with travel and adventure as one of the main themes.

Yes Man

Brings a message of saying yes to life, need I say more?

The Matrix

Philosophical mind fuck.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Quirky, interesting film concept, brilliant execution.

500 Days of Summer

Guess it can be considered a rom com... Can it? Either way, it's not like the others. Cool concept, interesting use of time line, or maybe it's just Zoe Deschanel. Who knows.
